What are my experiences

My journey towards being a nurse that cares for all begun when I first attended to nursing school in faculty of Medicine in Near East University (NEU). Starting from my first year I spend my free time in hospitals, infirmaries, caring homes as well as helping others with in-home care.

During nursery I found myself in more interest towards helping elders both physically and emotionally. Therefore, I decided to take my career in elderly care. It always gave me the greatest joy to make elderly people smile. This year marks my 12th year in both nursery and elderly care. I have worked as a nurse in intensive care units, operating rooms, emergency deparments and ambulance services of several hospitals. As a caring person, I have been involved in a numerous caring homes and in-home caring in multiple countries.

Especially for elderly people, it is highly recommended that they remain in their house to prevent any physical contact. If they need medical help, that can be brought to their houses easily.

As a graduate both from nursery and elderly care, I have the necessary knowledge to take care of anyone in-need. However, I am also aware the learning is never over thus I keep my mind open.
